# WoodEx Furniture Website - Design Blueprint
## Exact workspace.design UI/UX Replication for Furniture Business

### DESIGN OVERVIEW
**Style:** Modern, clean, professional with creative colorful headers
**Business:** Pakistan's leading custom office furniture manufacturer
**Target:** Corporate clients, businesses, designers, procurement managers
**Unique Value:** 1,200+ clients, 7-year warranty, custom manufacturing

## COLOR SCHEME & BRAND IDENTITY

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue:** #2563EB (workspace.design inspiration)
- **Accent Orange:** #F97316 (action/CTAs)
- **Deep Navy:** #1E293B (headers, professional look)
- **Light Gray:** #F8FAFC (backgrounds)

### Logo Colors
- **Primary Logo:** Black and Green color scheme
- **Logo Text:** "WoodEx" in black (#000000)
- **Logo Accent:** Green highlights/accents (#22C55E or #16A34A)

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Hero Headers:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter/Poppins)
- **Section Headers:** Semi-bold, colorful gradients
- **Body Text:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **CTA Buttons:** Bold, contrasting colors

### Creative Headers Design
- **Gradient Text:** Blue to Orange gradients for main headers
- **Large Scale:** 48-64px for hero headers
- **Typography Contrast:** Bold headers with lighter body text
- **Color Animation:** Subtle hover effects on headers

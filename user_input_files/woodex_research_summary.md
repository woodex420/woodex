# The Woodex Website Research Summary

**Research Date:** October 30, 2025  
**Website:** https://thewoodex.com/  
**Author:** MiniMax Agent

## Executive Summary

The Woodex is a premier luxury online furniture store based in New Delhi, India, specializing in both ready-made and custom-made furniture solutions. The company offers a 7-day custom furniture service and maintains a physical showroom alongside their e-commerce platform.

## Product Categories

The Woodex offers a comprehensive range of furniture categories:

1. **Chairs** - Various seating options including accent chairs and dining chairs
2. **Tables** - Dining tables, coffee tables, and accent tables
3. **Sofas** - Living room seating solutions
4. **Armchairs** - Individual seating pieces
5. **Beds** - Bedroom furniture including beds and headboards
6. **Storage** - Cabinets, dressers, and storage solutions
7. **Textiles** - Cushions, throws, and fabric accessories
8. **Lighting** - Table lamps, floor lamps, and decorative lighting
9. **Toys** - Children's furniture and play items
10. **Decor** - Decorative accessories and home accents
